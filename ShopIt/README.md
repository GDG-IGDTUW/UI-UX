## Description

ShopIt is a local marketplace shopping and delivery platform designed to connect
nearby businesses with customers through a seamless digital experience. The app
enables users to explore local stores, place orders, and track purchases while
supporting small and neighborhood businesses.

The platform focuses on convenience, trust, and accessibility through a clean
interface, OTP-verified authentication, and smooth shopping flows.

## Current Progress

The ShopIt application currently includes the following screens and features:

**Authentication (OTP-based Sign Up / Login):**
Allows users to securely sign up and log in using OTP verification for a
frictionless onboarding experience.

**Home Page:**
Displays active and on-the-way orders, recently purchased items, and order
status updates. Also highlights reviews left by users.

**Explore / Browse Page:**
Enables users to discover nearby stores and explore available products by
category.

**Product Detail Page:**
Displays item details, pricing, availability, and allows users to add products
to the cart or mark them as favorites.

**Cart & Checkout:**
Allows users to review selected items, update quantities, and place orders
easily.

**Profile Page:**
Shows user details, order history, recently bought items, favorite products,
and account preferences.

## Examples for Contributors

To improve usability, performance, and overall shopping experience,
contributors can work on the following areas:

### Home & Discovery Enhancements

* Improve visibility of on-the-way orders and delivery status
* Enhance recently bought and recommended items sections

### Authentication & Security

* Improve OTP input interactions and error handling
* Add resend OTP states and loading feedback

### Cart & Checkout Flow

* Simplify cart layout and pricing breakdown
* Improve checkout confirmation and success states

### Favorites & Order History

* Enhance favorite items management
* Improve recently bought items layout and reorder flow

### Visual & Accessibility Enhancements

* Improve typography hierarchy and spacing
* Ensure accessibility for touch targets, contrast, and readability

---

## Requirements for Contributors

To maintain quality and consistency, contributors should focus on:

**Usability:**
Ensure the interface is intuitive, fast, and easy to navigate for all users.

**Visual Hierarchy:**
Maintain consistent typography, spacing, and component usage for a clean and
organized shopping experience.

**Accessibility:**
Follow accessibility best practices including readable font sizes, sufficient
contrast, and clear interaction cues.

---

## Guidelines for Contribution

* Align all designs with ShopIt’s clean and user-friendly design language
* Prioritize speed, clarity, and ease of use
* Avoid clutter and unnecessary steps in shopping flows
* Test designs for responsiveness and cross-device consistency

---

This project offers contributors the opportunity to work on a real-world
commerce platform, strengthen mobile UX and product thinking skills, and
