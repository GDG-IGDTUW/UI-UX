# EcoCart (Mobile App UI/UX) – Project Overview

**Project Maintainer:** Deepanshi Dhiman | Shriya Sandilya

**Project Mentor:** Khwaish Diwan

---

## Description

EcoCart is a mobile application designed to help users track their carbon
footprint, make eco-friendly purchasing decisions, and stay motivated through
rewards and insights. The app combines sustainability tracking, personalized
recommendations, and gamification to encourage environmentally responsible
habits in a simple and engaging way.

## Features That Can Be Added or Improved

### 1. Onboarding

* Short onboarding flow explaining what EcoCart does
* Clear explanation of eco-scores and reward system
* Subtle animations for an engaging first-time experience

### 2. Authentication Enhancements

* Password visibility toggle
* Error states and validation feedback
* Improved spacing and accessibility

### 3. Carbon Tracking & Stats

* Category-wise emission breakdowns
* Interactive charts and tooltips
* Comparison with average user impact

### 4. Feed Improvements

* Clear eco-score explanations on product cards
* Eco-related badges for quick identification
* Filters such as low-carbon or category-based views

### 5. Rewards & Gamification

* Progress indicators toward rewards
* Tier-based reward system
* Limited-time eco challenges

### 6. Personalization

* User-defined sustainability goals
* Monthly and yearly impact summaries
* Achievement badges for milestones

## Design & Interaction Enhancements

* Maintain visual consistency across all screens
* Ensure accessibility and responsive layouts
* Improve visual hierarchy for better readability
* Add smooth transitions, micro-interactions, loading states, and empty states
* Prototype key user flows such as onboarding to reward earning

---

## Guidelines for Contributors

* Follow EcoCart’s minimal, calm, and eco-friendly design language
* Avoid visual clutter and prioritize usability
* Test designs for clarity, accessibility, and logical flow
* Ensure sustainability impact is clearly communicated throughout the app

---

## Project Goal

EcoCart aims to educate, motivate, and reward users for making sustainable
choices while delivering a smooth, intuitive, and engaging user experience.
